# 🚀 **2. Recent, High‑Quality Funded Startups in Solar Inspection (With Differentiators)**

## **🟦 A) Lab360 Solar (Australia) — $3.96M ARENA funding**

**Funding:** AU$3.96M federal funding (Sept 2025)  
**Tech:** Drone‑based **Daylight Photoluminescence (DPL)** imaging

*   Works **in full daylight**, unlike traditional EL/PL imaging which requires darkness
*   Identifies faults **down to individual PV cells**
*   Uses “optical switching” of modules via drones (unique)
*   Born out of **UNSW research**, 20+ years of luminescence R\&D

**Differentiator:**  
👉 **Only known tech that enables luminescence imaging without shutting down the plant or waiting for night.**  
This is a big leap over today’s thermal + RGB drone flights.

**Sources:**

*   ARENA funding announcement [\[arena.gov.au\]](https://arena.gov.au/news/arena-backs-drone-based-solar-inspection-start-up/)
*   pv magazine Australia coverage [\[pv-magazin...tralia.com\]](https://www.pv-magazine-australia.com/2025/09/15/lab360-lands-funding-to-help-get-drone-based-solar-inspection-tech-off-the-ground/)
*   PV Tech coverage [\[pv-tech.org\]](https://www.pv-tech.org/australian-start-up-wins-funding-to-commercialise-daylight-solar-inspection-technology/)

## **🟦 B) Raptor Maps (USA) — $35M Series C**

**Funding:** $35M Series C (Dec 2024)  
**Tech:**

*   Drone‑based thermal + RGB analysis
*   Asset modeling for entire PV plants
*   Computer vision to detect vegetation, string/inverter faults, thermal anomalies
*   End‑to‑end solar O\&M management platform

**Differentiator:**  
👉 **Full lifecycle asset management platform**, not just inspection.  
Large portfolios use it as a **system of record** for PV assets.

**Source:**

# 🟩 **5. Clear Differentiation Opportunities (Based on Market Gaps)**

### **1. Multi‑Modal Fusion Model (Thermal + RGB + EL/PL + Text Logs)**

No one has a **single unified model** that:

*   Fuses drone RGB
*   Thermal infrared
*   EL (electroluminescence)
*   PL (photoluminescence)
*   Inverter logs
*   IV curves

This would give **cell-level root-cause diagnostics**, not just fault detection.

***

### **2. AI Agent for Repair Prioritization (Cost × Impact)**

Everyone detects defects.  
No one tells you:

> “Fix these 47 modules first — highest ROI, 11.2% energy recovery.”

This insight is missing in platforms today.

***

### **3. Real‑time inspection → work‑order automation**

Raptor Maps does workflow, but limited automation.  
A fully agentic workflow:

*   Ingest inspection → auto‑triage → generate O\&M orders → optimize crew schedule.  
    This is not solved.

***

### **4. Low‑cost “plug‑and‑play” approach for SMB C\&I solar**

Existing platforms target:

*   Utility scale
*   Asset managers
*   Large EPCs

Nobody has a **cheap, quick** model for:

*   1–5 MW solar parks
*   Rooftop C\&I
*   Emerging markets

You could dominate this space.

***

### **5. A drone‑agnostic software stack**

Most startups bundle hardware + software.  
A purely software, drone‑agnostic offering (any DJI/Autel drone) would be a hit globally.

***

### **6. Vegetation + Soiling detection via multi-modal fusion**

Vegetation and soiling cost the industry **billions**, under-covered by CV today.

***

### **7. “Live digital twin” updated after every flight**

Raptor Maps does mapping, but not real-time dynamic updates.

***
