3. Contextual Regulatory Framework (Future)
The Australian Government is introducing the Renewable Electricity Guarantee of Origin (REGO) scheme. 
REGOs will eventually replace Large-scale Generation Certificates (LGCs) in 2030.
These REGOs are designed to facilitate time-stamped, hourly tracking, aligning with the "Granular Certificate" model promoted by EnergyTag.
